Coronavirus Updates

  • Lockdown

    3/23/2020

    Our Wokingham shop is temporarily closed but all of our European factories are still producing & delivering to the UK and we are still delivering to our customers where suitable, safe access allows. Our website is open though & if you have any questions on products or existing orders please email us at shop@stationfurniture.co.uk and we will respond to you as soon as possible. We look forward to resuming normal operation as soon as possible but please stay healthy and safe in the meantime.

  • Online Shop

    5/4/2020

    Hi, our retail shop still remains closed but our webshop is very much open. We are getting good deliveries from most of our suppliers now and they should be getting quicker over the next few weeks as lockdown eases over Europe.  If you want to talk to us the best way is still via email shop@stationfurniture.co.uk or call 07771 726190 We are looking to introduce a 'viewing by appointment ' service for those of you that would like to visit us when the shop is shut to minimise contact with other customers as well as some regular opening hours with measures put in place to make sure we all keep a safe distance from each other.  We are also purchasing some hand sanitising stations that can be used upon entering and leaving the shop and will hope to have these up and running in time for when we're open again. Stay safe and we look forward to seeing you again soon.

  • Re-opening

    5/18/2020

    We are aiming to re open on 1/6/2020 and at present we are putting in place hand sanitiser stations at both entrances for customers to use when entering and leaving our store. We will also be putting in a one way system to safely distance customers who may be visiting at the same time. We are now offering an appointment system where you are able to book a visit and view at leisure without other customers being here at the same time. please call (from 1/6/2020) 0118 9787740 or email (now) shop@station.furniture to book your visit. We will be available up until 9pm for these appointments. Most of our factories are getting back to normal, although reduced, operation & production but due to the lockdown lead times have reduced (in most cases) for delivery of customer orders. We still quote approx 8 weeks but in many cases this will now be much quicker! All of our furniture is European made in modern, safe factories who have all put distancing in place and are supporting workers who have had to isolate due to concerns of the virus but we will be checking with our other suppliers for accessories etc to make sure their factories are doing the same.

  • Government Advice

    5/28/2020

    Following advice from Boris we have to hold off reopening until 15th June, we are meeting customers by appointment from 1st though so please feel free to book a time and visit our shop at leisure without having to think about keeping distance from other customers. To book please email: shop@station.furniture

  • Lockdown Deliveries

    4/20/2020

    If you have placed an order with us we are still recieving deliveries from our European factories but delivery times have been extended due to distancing protocols that have been put in place inside the factories to protect the workers. Transport across Europe is also taking longer due to border checks at most countries but these should start to ease soon.  We do expect delivery times to start coming down for new orders placed through our website as most of the factories have caught up with lead times because of the lesser amount of orders from the other countries they supply. Mobitec our chair supplier & Sits, our sofa supplier are both fully operational and have good stocks of material to fulfil new orders placed. Our other factories also have a good supply of Oak to carry on making the fantastic tables & cabinet furniture they produce for us. Stay safe and see you soon!

Saturday are normal opening only, 10am to 5pm.
